## FAQ

### Is Guardian Link only for big moderation teams?

No. Smaller servers can still use it for cleaner alerting, logging, and safer moderation workflows.

### What is an alert channel?

An alert channel is where your moderators see important safety notifications in real time.

### What is a log channel?

A log channel is where detailed records are stored so staff can review actions later.

### Do I need both alert and log channels?

Not always, but using both keeps urgent alerts separate from long-form records.

### Why would invite logging matter?

Invite logging helps staff spot suspicious join patterns and investigate incidents faster.
