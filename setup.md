## First-Time Setup

### 1. Invite Guardian Link

Use the official invite button in the header or on the home page.

### 2. Place the bot role correctly

Open **Server Settings -> Roles** and drag the **Guardian Link** role above any roles the bot may need to act on.

### 3. Choose your mod channels

Create or pick channels for:

- **Alert channel**: where urgent safety alerts appear
- **Log channel**: where detailed moderation history is stored

### 4. Set channels in bot commands

Use your alert/log setup commands from [Commands](/guardian-link-docs/commands/) to connect the correct channels.

### 5. Test moderation flow

Run a test alert command and verify:

- Alert appears in your alert channel
- Detailed info appears in your log channel
- Staff can review the message clearly

### 6. Enable invite logging (optional but recommended)

Invite logging helps you see join patterns and suspicious invite activity. Turn it on and verify messages are posting in your selected log channel.

## Setup Checklist

- Bot role is placed high enough in **Server Settings -> Roles**
- Alert channel configured and receiving messages
- Log channel configured and receiving detailed records
- Invite logging enabled if your team wants join tracking
- Staff tested one full alert/mod workflow
